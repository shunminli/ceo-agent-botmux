# Novel Role Principle Sync

Synced cross-project novel production principles discovered in local BotMux story-team history back into the repository role configuration.

The role identities now explicitly cover:

- canon status separation: `final-canon`, `confirmed-principle`, `outline-candidate`, `publish-support`, and `publish-record`;
- mobile-platform readability: write so target readers quickly understand the action, responsibility, and consequence;
- short bridge sentences for complex reversals, instead of explanatory padding;
- `revised` / review packages are not `final` or Fanqie upload packages until Director approval and Validator gate pass;
- Fanqie author notes, chapter-end benefit images, cover prompts, and upload helpers are publishing support, not story canon.

`botmux-assets --write` should be used after this change to regenerate the three local novel bot workspace `AGENTS.md` files from the repository identities.
