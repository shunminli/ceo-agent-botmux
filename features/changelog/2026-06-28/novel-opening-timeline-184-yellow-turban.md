# Novel Opening Timeline 184 Yellow Turban

## Change

- The active Three Kingdoms novel project opening timeline is fixed to Eastern Han Zhongping year one, 184 CE.
- The opening stage is Yellow Turban Rebellion just beginning, before warlord fragmentation.
- The Director, Creative, and Validator identities now block opening chapters that prematurely use Dong Zhuo's takeover, the anti-Dong Zhuo coalition, or established warlord fragmentation as background.

## Impact

- Chapter 001 can start from county-level crisis while the Han commandery/county bureaucracy still has real pressure.
- Later Dong Zhuo and warlord arcs remain available as future-stage escalation after Lu Heng has built county and commandery-level momentum.
